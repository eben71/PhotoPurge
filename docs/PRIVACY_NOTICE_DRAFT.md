## Purpose

PhotoPrune is a validation MVP that analyzes user-selected Google Photos items to identify potential duplicates for review. It is designed to prioritize trust, predictability, and cost control over completeness or automation.

## Data We Access (Phase 2)

- User-selected media items via the Google Photos Picker API.
- Metadata provided by Google Photos (e.g., filename, timestamps, dimensions) when available.

## Data We Do NOT Access (Phase 2)

- Full library scans or non-selected photos.
- Embeddings or semantic representations.

## How We Use Data

- Generate candidate groups of potential duplicates.
- Present review-only results to the user.
